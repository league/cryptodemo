Public Key Cryptography Demo

Public key crypto systems are one of the most remarkable and practical inventions in the history of codes, because they don’t rely on a shared secret. Two keys work together: a fully public one, and one that is known only to you. Find a friend and enjoy this little demonstration:

  • Register
  • Log in
  • Send message
  • Read messages

Register an identity

  1. Enter your name: OK
  2. Move your mouse around to seed the random number generator. You can stop moving when this number reaches zero →
  3. Choose the size of your key: Larger keys are better, but can take much longer to calculate.
  4. This may take a few moments… Public key:
  5. the private key and store it someplace safe. It cannot be retrieved or reset if you lose it.
